Welcome to Fridge Mates

Join us in the journey to reduce food waste and build a community of sharing and sustainability.

Our Mission

Fridge Mates is dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting members of a community through a social application. Users can post the contents of their refrigerators, propose trades, or offer to take expiring food. Our tech-driven platform focuses on doing good for the environment and fostering a sense of unity.
